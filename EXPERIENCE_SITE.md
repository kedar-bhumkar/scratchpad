## Adding the Patient Portal Component

To add the `patientPortalHome` component to your site:

1. Go to Setup > Digital Experiences > All Sites
2. Click "Builder" next to "Patient Portal"
3. Navigate to the Home page
4. In the components panel, search for "patientPortalHome"
5. Drag and drop the component onto the page
6. Save and publish the changes
